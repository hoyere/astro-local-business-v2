Our Routine Maintenance program applies repeatable, documented processes to every pool visit. Each technician follows the same 27-point protocol — no variance, no guesswork.

## What's Included

Every weekly visit covers surface debris removal, wall brushing, vacuum passes, tile line cleaning, and a full equipment check. We log results in our system so you can track trends over time.

## Why It Works

- **Documented Processes**: Every step is recorded and timestamped
- **Consistent Execution**: Same protocol regardless of which technician arrives
- **Proactive Alerts**: We flag developing issues before they become expensive repairs
- **Transparent Reporting**: Access your pool's maintenance history anytime
