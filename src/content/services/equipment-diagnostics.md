Equipment failures don't happen suddenly — they degrade gradually. Our Equipment Diagnostics program catches declining performance early by benchmarking your pool systems against manufacturer baselines.

## What We Track

Every visit includes pump amperage readings, filter differential pressure, heater delta-T measurements, and automation system status checks. We compare each reading against your pool's historical norms.

## Preventive Approach

- **Trend Detection**: We spot declining pump flow before you notice weak jets
- **Lifecycle Management**: We track component age and recommend replacement windows
- **Vendor-Neutral Assessments**: Our recommendations are based on data, not brand partnerships
- **Emergency Prevention**: Catching a failing pump capacitor costs far less than a flooded equipment pad
