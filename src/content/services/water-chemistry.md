Water chemistry is not an art — it's a system. Our technicians use calibrated testing instruments and standardized dosing formulas to maintain your pool water within optimal parameters.

## Our Approach

We test seven chemical parameters at every visit and feed the results into our dosing calculator. The system accounts for pool volume, recent weather, bather load, and historical trends to recommend precise chemical additions.

## Parameters We Monitor

- **pH Balance**: Maintained between 7.2 and 7.6 for swimmer comfort and equipment longevity
- **Total Alkalinity**: Buffered to prevent pH drift
- **Free Chlorine / Sanitizer**: Optimized for consistent disinfection
- **Calcium Hardness**: Managed to protect plaster and equipment surfaces
